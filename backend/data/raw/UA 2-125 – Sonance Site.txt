UA Series Amplifiers
UA 2-125

UA 2-125 is an ultra-compact 2-channel DSP amplifier delivering 125W per channel (250W in bridged Power Sharing Mode). Featuring analog and digital inputs, local zone audio, flexible mounting with a T-slot chassis, and easy setup with SonARC for UA Series Amplifiers app, it's designed for performance and versatility in any space.
