SONANCE
UA LOCAL ZONE AMPLIFIERS
UA 2-125 | UA 2-125 ARC

Sonance UA amplifiers provide robust DSP amplification in an ultra-compact form factor to support a wide range of applications. UA amps are designed for local zone applications like bedroom and TV music, outdoor entertainment, or any two-channel location that may have space limitations. Their unique T-slot heatsink chassis allows for flexible installation into the TV back box, structured wiring can, or the Atelix type outdoor enclosure. Select from the standard or ARC model, each with fully capable DSP and Bluetooth-enabled app setup.
